About Us
Premier Network & Infrastructure Experts in Alberta
MEGACOM is the premier network and infrastructure expert in Alberta. We cover the entire process from design consultation, installation, and maintenance. Giving our clients the peace of mind and complete satisfaction with fiber optics installation is our ultimate goal. We offer full-service fiber solutions all across Canada.

Experienced Team

We have been in business since 2011 and have grown into a very experienced team. With over 30 years of experience in the telecommunications field, we have got everything covered.

We offer all aspects of data and voice setup in residential, commercial, and industrial sites. We have completed work at schools, warehouses and oil sites.
